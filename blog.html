Reimagining Blogging with the Worldview WordPress Theme

We have been working hard on the product side of things on the Worldview Theme for WordPress. It is a gorgeously-designed blog theme with bold cover photos and modern, crisp typography. We spent over 3 months tweaking Worldview to ensure it meets the highest-quality design standards we strive to achieve for our team. Our goal was to create a theme for bloggers that injects emotion and beauty into their writing and as a result, Worldview was born.

Big Plans for Worldview

In addition to this theme release, we are also working with Nick Haskins on a child theme that will introduce a beautiful marriage between Aesop Story Engine and Worldview, allowing writers and authors to craft stories with many of the editorial elements you might expect to find in a New York Times piece. In addition, we are also working on a child theme called Largo, which will offer a more Medium.com-style experience with full-width content/cover photos and a true distraction-free reading experience.

Worldview is now available on UpThemes.com and for users of WordPress.com.
